Shorten a URL

Pro Tip: Use from your URL Bar

You can create short links directly from Chrome's address bar without visiting this page.

  • Go to Chrome Settings → Search engine → Manage search engines and site search.
  • Under "Site search", click Add.
  • Fill in the fields:
    • Search engine: Your Shortener Name
    • Shortcut: link (or your domain)
    • URL with %s in place of query: https://your-domain.com/add.html?q=%s
  • Now, type link in your address bar, press Space, and then paste your long URL, optionally followed by a space and your custom slug.
