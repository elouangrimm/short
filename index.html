Pro Tip

You can create short links directly from Chrome's omnibox without visiting this page!

  • Go to Chrome Settings → Search engine → Manage search engines and site search.
  • Under "Site search", click Add.
  • Fill in the fields:
    • Search engine: short
    • Shortcut: link (or whatever you want to remember)
    • URL with %s in place of query: https://short.elouan.xyz/add?q=%s
  • Now, type link in your address bar, press Space, and then paste your long URL, optionally followed by a space and your custom slug!
